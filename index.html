Our Burwood family dentists offer a spa-like, luxury feel with a down-to-earth dental team. Here you'll find multilingual providers who are focused on gentle, quality care . And for our senior patients who cannot travel, mobile services are available.

About Our
Mission

Here you'll find comfortable care that is specially tailored to your individual needs. By communicating closely with each patient, we are able to provide a higher level of personalized dentistry that fits your health background, goals and lifestyle.

Experiencing a dental emergency? Our accessible team is available after hours by contacting our practice. We can walk you through what to do and arrange to see you as soon as possible.

Flexible Scheduling
and Payment Options

Feel free to book your appointment online. We work with several insurance plans, offering low and gap-free payments. Flexible financing is also available on all services.

Our key patient focus is tailored around family and emergency dentistry. By offering timely care for all ages , we can better ensure a positive experience as your family grows and limit the need for more advanced treatments.

Something
for Everyone

From children's dental checkups to cosmetic dental veneers or implants, we have the resources you need for your smile to function at its finest. If you have questions or concerns about your oral health, our dentists will co-plan your care alongside of you, tailoring the treatment experience to your specific circumstances.

We offer
Home Visits

We are delighted to have recently started doing home visits (mobile dentistry) for seniors and disabled patients and will be expanding our service to all ages.

We hope to be able to provide services to most suburbs as this will assist people who are elderly and those who cannot make it to the dentist due to health reasons.

If you are searching for an experienced mobile dentist in Burwood contact us today to discuss your circumstances. Please note that at this stage we do have a waiting list for this service.
